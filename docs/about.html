ASHTONCORP SOLUTIONS is a company providing Accounting, System Automation and other financial services to various private, corporate and public sector clients since 1999. The company evolved into a Limited Liability Company in 2010, registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act of 1990 to meet accounting and financial information requirements of multidisciplinary entrepreneurs and Government ministries and agencies by providing automated accounting system, Taxation, financial advisory services, Auditing and investigation, Human resource management and payroll, serving as their virtual Finance, Accounts and Strategy department.  Our professional teams have over fifteen year’s practical experience in all facets of accounting, system automation and financial advisory support. We believe that when our client prospers, we prosper; therefore, we are always eager to provide all requisite advisory and technical supports required for their growth.

We provide services for customers in both the public and private sector and continuously evaluate their peculiarities to establish their needs and requirements. This information is constantly updated in our customer service experience data and gives us a clear advantage over competition in service delivery. The company goes the extra mile to headhunt experts in any area we are short staffed to ensure that our service delivery is by no means diluted and do what is most important to us, deliver quality on time and keep our customers happy.
